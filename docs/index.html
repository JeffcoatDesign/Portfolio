Welcome to my Portfolio

Hello, I am Jacob Jeffcoat, a detail-oriented programmer with experience implementing advanced systems in simulated environments. GOAP, A*, and Delaunay Triangulation are some of the advanced systems I have implemented in my projects. Most of my projects are built as video games in order to maintain a small scope and allow for more volunteers for testing and feedback while I explore the technical aspects of simulation. I look forward to creating integrated systems within simulated environments.
