## Overview
Embry-Riddle's Simulation Science, Games, and Animation department sponsored a scavenger hunt for its students who were attending the Game Developer's Conference in 2024 and 2025. As the program assistant and a proficient programmer, I was tasked with building a way to gather submissions and keep score for this scavenger hunt. The application had to be self-hosted and provide students a way to view and interact with submissions. Naturally, my partner and I decided that it was best to build a Discord application because the students already had a community on the platform. When we had our inital prototype built, we conducted a small test with about 10 users, which led to several important improvements before going live. The first version had some hiccups that led to improvements on the next.
