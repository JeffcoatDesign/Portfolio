## Overview
Horse Meadow is a small proof of concept that acted as a dive into database design with SQL. I developed it as a team of two, where we aomed to apply model-view-controller architecture. While my partner created art and our MySQL database, I wrote the PHP queries and integrated them into the game itself. I made sure that the user intface responded to the changes in the database.
